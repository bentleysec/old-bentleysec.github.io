To find the answer to the question you will need to look at the Inspector. Go back to the "Try It!" form on the previous page and right-click on the Go! button and click on Inspect. This reveals the html code behind the page. Look for the <form> tag and it will include method. The method will tell you if it is a POST or a GET.

To find the magic number, while on the Inspector to find the form method, click on the search and search for "magic"
